# Product Rules

See `AI_CONTEXT.md` for the full MVP scope, business vocabulary, order statuses and entity list.

## Enforcement (apply to every change)

- Do not introduce online payment, delivery, loyalty program or multi-merchant marketplace without explicit request.
- Keep **Kadhia** as the business term — never replace it with a generic "panier".
- Currency is always TND.
- UI must support French and Arabic; Arabic requires RTL.
- Separate shared product reference from merchant offer — the merchant owns price, availability and visibility.
- The QR code shop access is the central entry point for customers.
- Pickup must keep a secure double-validation step.
