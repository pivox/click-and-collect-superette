# Product Rules

These rules apply to the full project.

## MVP constraints

- Keep the MVP focused on shop QR access, catalog, Kadhia, pickup slot, merchant validation, preparation and pickup.
- Do not introduce online payment unless explicitly requested.
- Do not introduce delivery unless explicitly requested.
- Do not introduce a multi-merchant marketplace cart unless explicitly requested.
- Do not introduce complex warehouse stock in the MVP.

## Tunisia context

- Currency is TND.
- UI must support French and Arabic.
- Arabic UI may require RTL.
- Product names, brands, formats and volumes are critical for search.

## Vocabulary

- Keep **Kadhia** as the business term.
- Use supérette, marchand, client, rendez-vous and retrait consistently.

## Product reference

- Separate shared product reference from merchant offer.
- The merchant offer owns price, availability and visibility.
- The shared product reference may contain name, brand, category, format, volume and unit.
